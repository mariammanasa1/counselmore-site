Our Services

CounselMore offers a comprehensive suite of tools designed specifically for college counselors. Here's how our platform can transform your practice:

Student Profile Management

Keep track of all essential student information in one place, including academic history, extracurricular activities, and personal development notes. Our intuitive profiles make it easy to quickly access student information and track progress over time.

  • Custom fields to track what matters most to your practice
  • Document storage for transcripts, essays, and more
  • Easy-to-read progress indicators
  • Student and parent portal access

Meeting Management

Streamline your appointment scheduling, note-taking, and follow-up processes. Our system automatically generates meeting summaries and action items, saving you valuable time.

  • Integrated calendar with automatic reminders
  • Template-based note-taking tools
  • AI-assisted meeting summaries
  • Automatic follow-up task creation

College List Building

Help students find their perfect match with our comprehensive college database and smart filtering tools. Create balanced lists with reach, target, and safety schools based on real admission data.

  • Database of over 3,000 colleges and universities
  • Advanced filtering by location, size, programs, and more
  • Side-by-side comparison tools
  • Historical admission data integration

Application Tracking

Keep everyone on track with deadline management and task assignments. Monitor application status across multiple platforms from one central dashboard.

  • Visual timeline of key deadlines
  • Task assignment for students, parents, and counselors
  • Status tracking across Common App, Coalition, and direct applications
  • Document submission verification
